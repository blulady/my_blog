About Sarah

A Developer's Tale

“No dragon can resist the fascination of riddling talk and of wasting time trying to understand it.”

- J. R. R. Tolkien


Until 2020 I was a business owner who started growing my acupuncture practice shortly after graduating from my master's program. I ran my own marketing, utilizing Google AdWords and Google Analytics. I maintained my own website updating design elements and content. I also integrated my electronic health records service into my website so people could submit their insurance for verification after I taught myself medical billing. When the pandemic hit, I had health concerns because of underlying conditions and my response to catching covid was not great. So I went back to what I had wanted to do before attending my master's program, which was work with computers. So now I am a software developer who loves learning and making magic. Logic has a plethora of applications and this is an expressive one. I am experienced with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and Python. I would love to make magic for your projects.
